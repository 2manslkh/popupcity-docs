# Risk Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation and mitigation of potential risks in pop-up city operations.

## Risk Categories

### Operational Risks

- Infrastructure failures
- Supply chain disruptions
- Staff management
- Resource shortages

### Safety Risks

- Health emergencies
- Natural disasters
- Security incidents
- Crowd management

### Financial Risks

- Budget overruns
- Revenue shortfalls
- Insurance coverage
- Legal liabilities
